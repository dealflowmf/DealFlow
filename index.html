Connect Your Gmail Inbox

Forward deal marketing emails to a dedicated Gmail address. This app reads that inbox, extracts key deal data with AI, and sends you a daily digest.

  1. 1Create a Gmail address to use as your deal drop box — e.g. [email protected]
  2. 2In Outlook, create a rule to forward broker emails to that Gmail
  3. 3Sign in below with that Gmail to connect
  4. 4Enter your real email to receive the daily summary
From Google Cloud Console → Credentials → OAuth 2.0 Client IDs
This app requests read-only Gmail access (gmail.readonly) and the ability to send email on your behalf for the daily summary (gmail.send). No emails are stored on any server — all data stays in your browser.
